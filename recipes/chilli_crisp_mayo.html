Chilli Crisp Mayo

Description

This Chili Crisp Mayo blends creamy mayonnaise with crunchy, spicy chili oil for a bold and savory kick.

It's perfect as a dip, sandwich spread, or a flavorful topping for fries and rice bowls.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon spicy chilli crisp, such as Lao Gan Ma

Steps

  1. Place mayonnaise and chili crisp into the bowl of a mini food processor.
  2. Blend until smooth and evenly combined, scraping down the sides of the bowl. Alternately, you can stir the mayonnaise and chili crisp together in a bowl.
